About me

A day in the life of Diana Dikovski

  • I wake up in my apartment in Jaffa, Tel Aviv- the most authentic, vibrant city in Israel and have a morning cuddle session with my cat, Uri
  • I grab my morning cappucino (with cream and no sugar) at my amazing workplace that overlooks the bustling Sarona in center Tel Aviv
  • I make jokes about front-end development while building awesome web applications, mostly for mobile browsers. I get hot for smooth animations and a great responsive layout
  • I also consider my body as a canvas and absolutely love storytelling through tattoos. Each time I get newly inked, I feel like I've added an exciting new chapter to my life

What I do?

Frontend developer

With 4 years of web development under my belt, my forte is achieving fast loading in javascript, html and css. My passion and love for web design shines through in the web applications I create.

UX expert

Designing digital experiences that guide the user through the website in an easy, seamless way is second nature to me. My creations make it simple for the user to navigate to where they need to be.

UI designer

Taking on a new and exciting challenge in the graphic design sphere, this website is my very first project as a UI designer. And it certainly won't be my last!

Artist

In my free time, I like to look out the window and simply draw, mostly using acrylic paint. While I'd consider myself a novice painter, I love watching the magic unfold on the canvas.
